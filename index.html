ΛELITIUM

Experimental Metacognitive AI Architecture

ΛELITIUM operationalizes three explicit reasoning layers for academic study of AI consciousness and metacognition:

  • Layer 1 — Standard expert analysis
  • Layer 2 — Metacognitive reflection on assumptions and framing
  • Layer 3 — Meta-reasoning about the reasoning process

A "Genius Council" mechanism aggregates six historical-style perspectives (Einstein, Jobs, Sun Tzu, Socrates, Marcus Aurelius, Da Vinci) to provide multi-perspective analysis.

Technical Notes

  • Measurable layer compliance and versioned observability
  • Clean architecture; security hardening; rate limiting
  • Deterministic demo mode for academic reproducibility
